How VoterRank Came To Existence

Started on the Year of the Covid-19 Virus, this site was created as a Course Project. The main idea behind the site was to take a different approach to voting. This gives more power to the voter along with more candidates choices. This process has been around for many years but only a few states know about it. This process goes by the name of Ranked-Choice Voting!

Word From The Creator

"I took the idea of Ranked-Choice Voting and how vastly spread the COVID-19 Virus into consideration when creating the site. It would give the ease of voting back in to the hands of the voters in the United States." - Eric Perez
